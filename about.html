Who I am

I'm Srikant Rochan Ragghu, a full stack developer who turns ideas into clean, usable products for the web. I enjoy the whole loop, from sketching an interface to wiring the logic behind it.

I work at TAD N.V., a Suriname-born digital agency in Paramaribo that builds products, brands and platforms for clients worldwide such as shipping e-commerce, mobile apps, in-house SaaS and campaigns that reach millions.

I care about thoughtful UI & UX, readable code and work that feels fast and polished. Outside of class, I build side projects to learn by doing usually HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Next.js, Tailwind and TanStack. Right now I'm sharpening my craft through study, personal builds and real products at work.
